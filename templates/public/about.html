About Korje Hasanah Group (KHG)

Learn about our motivation, mission, vision, and how we operate with transparency and trust.

Our Motivation

KHG was founded to provide an ethical, community-driven alternative to traditional interest-based lending. We believe in mutual support, transparency, and empowering members to achieve financial stability without exploitation.

Our Mission

To foster a trusted community where members can borrow and lend interest-free, share resources, and collectively uplift each other through responsible financial practices.

Our Vision

We envision a world where financial assistance is guided by ethics and solidarity, enabling communities to thrive without dependency on interest or unfair practices.

How KHG Runs

KHG operates as a transparent microfinance platform. Membership applications are reviewed by admins, loans are approved through democratic voting, and fund histories are visible to all members. This ensures accountability, fairness, and continuous trust within our community.
