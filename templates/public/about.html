About Korje Hasanah Group (KHG)

We are a faith-driven microfinance platform with the mission to transform society by eliminating interest-based borrowing and empowering communities with ethical, interest-free financial solutions.

Our Motivation

In today’s economy, most financial systems operate on interest-based lending — a practice we believe is ethically wrong and harmful to society. KHG was founded to create a sustainable, transparent, and ethical alternative. Our mission is not just to provide interest-free loans, but to build a movement that dismantles the current interest-based economy and replaces it with a cooperative, faith-aligned system where financial help is a right, not a profit-driven commodity.

We are inspired by the vision of a society where people live without the burden of riba (interest), and where financial support fosters entrepreneurship, dignity, and mutual care.

Our Mission

To destroy the current interest-based economy by creating an alternative platform for ethical microfinance that:

  • Provides interest-free loans to members in need.
  • Encourages entrepreneurial ventures.
  • Promotes financial independence without exploitation.
  • Creates a community built on trust, transparency, and cooperation.

Our Vision

To build a world where financial services are free from riba, enabling communities to thrive without reliance on haram banking systems. We envision a future where every member becomes a self-reliant entrepreneur empowered to achieve their goals, supported by a strong community foundation.

How KHG Operates

Korje Hasanah Group functions as a transparent, community-based microfinance network. Membership is open to anyone who shares our values and is committed to our mission. Members apply with a signed oath paper, agreeing to abide by our ethical and operational guidelines.

Loans are issued based on member applications, repayment history, and community voting. Our system allows members to actively participate in approving loans, ensuring fairness and accountability. All donations, repayments, and loan histories are fully transparent and available to members through their dashboards.

Our operation is guided by these principles:

  • Transparency in all processes.
  • Mutual accountability among members.
  • Strict adherence to ethical lending principles.
  • Support for member entrepreneurship.

Our Core Values

  • Faith & Ethics: All actions are rooted in Islamic principles, avoiding riba and exploitation.
  • Transparency: Open access to all transactions and decision-making processes.
  • Mutual Support: Members supporting each other financially and socially.
  • Empowerment: Helping members achieve independence and financial growth.
  • Community: Building a network of trust and cooperation.
