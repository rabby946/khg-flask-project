How to Use the KHG Platform

Please read the following instructions carefully to make the most out of your membership and our services.

  1. Membership Application: Navigate to the Apply Membership page and fill out the form with your accurate details. Your information is secure with us.
  2. Approval Process: Once submitted, your application will be reviewed by our admin team. You will receive an email and a dashboard notification upon approval.
  3. Accessing Your Dashboard: After approval, log in to access your personal dashboard. Here you can view your membership history, active loans, and payment schedules.
  4. Applying for a Loan: From your dashboard, go to the Loan Application section, fill out your request, and submit. Ensure all required fields are completed for faster processing.
  5. Voting on Loans: As a member with voting rights, you can review pending loan applications from other members and cast your vote, contributing to our transparent, community-driven process.
  6. Loan Repayment: Keep track of your active loans in your dashboard. Timely repayments are crucial for maintaining a good standing within the community and improving your member score.
  7. Stay Updated: Check your dashboard regularly for notifications regarding application status, payment reminders, and important community announcements.
  8. Contact Support: If you encounter any issues or have questions, please use the Contact page to reach out to our dedicated support team.
